Is Medigap right for you?

Medicare Supplement tends to be the better fit if any of these apply to your situation.

You use healthcare frequently

Ongoing conditions, regular specialist visits, or chronic illness make predictable costs more valuable than low premiums.

You travel inside the U.S.

Medigap lets you see any doctor that accepts Medicare anywhere in the country — no network, no service area restrictions.

Your doctors aren't in MA networks

If your preferred doctors or hospital don't participate in Medicare Advantage networks, Medigap is often the only way to keep them.

You want zero billing surprises

With Plan G, once you meet your $257 Part B deductible, Medicare and Medigap cover the rest. No surprise bills for covered services.
