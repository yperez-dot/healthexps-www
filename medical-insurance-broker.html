Why Is It Free?

Insurance carriers pay us a commission when you enroll. This is how the industry works — whether you use a broker or enroll directly with a carrier, the commission is already built into the plan's cost.

The difference? When you work with us, you get expert guidance, plan comparisons, and ongoing support at no additional cost. The carrier pays us the same commission whether you enroll through us or on your own — so why not get the help?

We make more when you're happy and stay enrolled. Our goal is to find you a plan that actually works for your needs — not the plan with the highest commission. That's what builds trust and long-term relationships.
