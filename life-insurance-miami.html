Which type of life insurance is right for you?

Every family's situation is different. Here's a plain-English breakdown of the three most common types.

⏱️
Term Life Insurance
Coverage for a fixed period — typically 10, 20, or 30 years. If you pass away during the term, your beneficiaries receive a tax-free payout. The most affordable option for most working families.
✅ Best for: Young families, mortgage protection, income replacement
🏦
Whole Life Insurance
Permanent coverage that never expires. Builds a cash value over time that you can borrow against. Higher premiums than term, but your coverage and rates are locked in forever.
✅ Best for: Estate planning, permanent protection, wealth transfer
🕊️
Final Expense Insurance
Smaller whole life policies designed to cover funeral costs, medical bills, and end-of-life expenses. Easy to qualify for — no medical exam required. Available for seniors aged 50–85.
✅ Best for: Seniors, covering burial costs, no-exam approval
