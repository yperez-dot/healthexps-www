2026 IRMAA Calculator

Find out exactly what you'll pay for Medicare based on your income.

Medicare uses your income from 2 years ago to determine if you owe an IRMAA surcharge on top of your standard Part B and Part D premiums. Enter your 2024 Modified Adjusted Gross Income (MAGI) below to see exactly what you'll pay in 2026. Results are estimates — if your income has changed significantly, you may be able to appeal your IRMAA. A licensed broker can help you understand your options at no cost.

⚠️

Medicare uses your tax return from 2 years ago. For 2026, they use your 2024 income.
