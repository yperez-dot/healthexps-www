Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about the Medicare plan finder tool.

Is the Medicare plan finder really free?

Yes. The tool is completely free to use, and there is no cost to enroll in a plan through our licensed brokers. We are compensated by the insurance carriers when you choose to enroll — never by you. There is no obligation to buy anything.

Which Medicare plans can I compare?

You can compare 2026 Medicare Advantage (Part C) plans, standalone Part D prescription drug plans, and Medicare Supplement (Medigap) plans available in your Florida ZIP code. We represent 14 carriers including Humana, UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, and Wellcare.

Do I need to enter personal information to use the tool?

You will need your ZIP code and date of birth to see plan options. You can also add your prescriptions and preferred doctors to get more accurate cost estimates. We do not require your Social Security number or Medicare ID number to compare plans.

When can I switch my Medicare plan?

Most people can change Medicare plans during the Annual Enrollment Period (October 15 – December 7) for coverage starting January 1. You may also qualify for a Special Enrollment Period if you recently moved, lost other coverage, became eligible for Extra Help, or your current plan is leaving Florida. Call us at 1-800-380-6821 to find out if you qualify.

What if I need help understanding the results?

Call us at 1-800-380-6821 or email info@healthexps.com. Our licensed bilingual Medicare brokers in Doral will walk you through your options in English or Spanish, answer your questions, and help you enroll in the plan that fits your needs and budget. There is no charge for this service.
