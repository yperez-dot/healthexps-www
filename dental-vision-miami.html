What dental & vision plans typically cover

Coverage varies by plan and carrier, but here's what's commonly included across the plans we offer in South Florida:

🦷
Dental Coverage
2 cleanings and exams per year — usually $0
X-rays — routine and panoramic
Fillings and basic restorative work
Extractions and oral surgery
Crowns, bridges, and dentures (major services)
Root canals (basic or major tier)
Orthodontics on select plans
Emergency dental care
👓
Vision Coverage
Annual eye exam — usually $0 or low copay
Frames allowance (typically $100–$200/year)
Prescription lenses — single, bifocal, progressive
Contact lens allowance as alternative to glasses
Lens enhancements (anti-glare, UV, etc.) on select plans
LASIK discounts on some plans
Additional pair discounts mid-year on premium plans
