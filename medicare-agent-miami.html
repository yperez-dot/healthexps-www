Our story

The Health Experts Insurance was built on a simple belief: every person deserves a Medicare broker who understands them — their language, their doctors, their community.

When Yahoska and Katy founded The Health Experts Insurance in 2019, they saw a gap in South Florida's Medicare market: a largely Spanish-speaking senior community being served by brokers who didn't speak their language, didn't know their neighborhoods, and didn't stay available after the enrollment was done.

They built THEI to be different. Independent — so they could always show you every option, from every carrier, without any hidden allegiance. Bilingual — not as a translation service, but as brokers who grew up in this culture and understand the nuances that matter. Local — with an office in Doral at the heart of the community they serve.

Today, THEI serves hundreds of clients across Miami-Dade and South Florida — helping Medicare-eligible seniors compare plans, navigate cost-saving programs like MSP and Extra Help, and make confident decisions about their healthcare.

What makes us different

There are a lot of Medicare brokers in South Florida. Here's why our clients choose us and stay with us.

100% independent

We represent 14+ carriers and have no exclusivity agreement with any of them. Your best plan is our only agenda.

Truly bilingual

English and Spanish — not as a translation, but as our first languages. We understand the community we serve at a cultural level.

Local Doral office

We're not a call center. We're in Doral, in the community. Meet us in person, or we'll come to you.

Year-round support

We don't disappear after enrollment. Billing questions, plan changes, doctor issues — we're here all year.

We do the research

We check your doctors, your medications, and your out-of-pocket costs across every plan before making a recommendation.

Always free

Our service costs you nothing. We're compensated by the insurance carriers — your premium is the same with or without a broker.
